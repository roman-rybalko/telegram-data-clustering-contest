ROUTE TO RECOVERY

Rangers ace Jordan Jones back in training after injury against Celtic

22 Nov 2019, 07:31 by David Irvine

JORDAN JONES is back in Rangers training after 12 weeks out.

The Light Blues winger has been sidelined for a significant period of time after injuring himself in a wild challenge on Moritz Bauer at Ibrox.

Jones came on for Steven Gerrard's side in the last quarter of the 2-0 Old Firm loss and was given his marching orders for a reckless challenge on the Stoke City loanee in the closing minutes.

He was then spotted leaving Ibrox on crutches as a result of the injury.

But Jones has given a positive report on his return to fitness on Instagram.
