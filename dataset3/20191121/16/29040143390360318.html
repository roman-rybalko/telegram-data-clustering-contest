How to find the best Black Friday deals on smart plugs for Alexa and Google Home

As far as smart home gadgets go, smart plugs are among the least-complicated. That said, there are a few factors to keep in mind that'll ensure you buy one that fits you and your lifestyle. For starters, the design of the smart plug you buy is very important. All of these plugs will go into any wall outlet in your home, but each one takes up a different amount of space. Some smart plugs are compact enough that you can plug two on top of each other, whereas others are a bit bulkier and take up the entire outlet space on your wall. You'll also find some smart plugs that take the shape of a power strip — allowing you to plug in a variety of things that can be controlled with your preferred virtual assistant. A lot of smart plugs also come with energy-monitoring tools, allowing you to easily see how much energy each plug is using so you can possibly get a cheaper utility bill each month. Lastly, seeing as most of the smart plugs on this list work with both Alexa and Google Assistant, you can control them with your voice whether you have Amazon Echo or Google Home speakers in your home.
