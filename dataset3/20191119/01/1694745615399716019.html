This year welcomed some of the most exciting beauty gadgets and tools. (Some were so innovative, in fact, they were awarded a first-ever Power Your Pretty Award.) These tech-forward products make the best gifts for your friends who are always searching for the next big thing in beauty.

Not only do they look futuristic, but they are also effective in streamlining your hair, makeup, nail, and skincare routines. On this list, you'll find facial massagers that buzz away puffiness, hair tools that nix the extra wiring, and one nail art attachment that solves an age-old manicure problem. Check out the best high-tech beauty gifts of 2019 ahead.

Pro Makeup Brush Cleaner & Dryer Kit

With the Pro Makeup Brush Cleaner & Dryer Kit ($30), there is no excuse for putting off cleaning your brushes. The device attaches to the brush and spins it quickly in a bowl of water and soap.

Snow Fox Skin Care Rose Quartz Facial Roller

The Snow Fox Skin Care Rose Quartz Facial Roller ($24) is one of this year's Power Your Pretty Award winners. It's made of 100-percent rose quartz, and allows you to roll away stress and morning puffiness. The stone is believed to promote luck in romance, charm, and spiritual healing, making it the perfect beauty gift for self-care.

Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare SpectraLite Faceware Pro

The Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare SpectraLite Faceware Pro ($435) uses red and blue LED lights to deplete acne-causing bacteria and smooth out lines — all while making you look like an extra in Star Wars.

Olive and June The Poppy

The patented Olive and June The Poppy ($16) is a Power Your Pretty Award recipient for how it solved the age-old problem that is painting your nails with your non-dominant hand. The first-of-its-kind invention works by simply wiggling the silicone attachment onto the cap of your favorite bottle of nail polish, and using it to get a tighter grip on the brush as you paint on the color.

Supersonic Hair Dryer Gift Edition With Red Case

Beauty editors and hairstylists alike agree: the Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer was the most innovative launch of 2016. After all, it took a team of over 100 engineers to develop it. Its highlights include a lightweight body (it weighs in just above a pound without attachments), a quiet motor, and the ability to cut your dry time in half. This blow dryer already has a sleek, futuristic body but is getting an equally sophisticated makeover for this Winter. The Supersonic Hair Dryer Gift Edition ($399) features red packaging in place of its signature magenta and sits in a leather case of the same hue.

Quip Toothbrush

The Quip Toothbrush ($40) thoroughly cleanses teeth using microvibrations. Just press its singular button to set off the vibrations and a two-minute timer. It's a minimalist's dream with its sleek design and no need for a bulky charger.

NuFace Mini Shimmer All Night Collection

The NuFace Mini Shimmer All Night Collection ($199) contains a rose-gold-armored device that uses microcurrents to temporarily sculpt the contours of your face.

DermaFlash 2.0 Facial Exfoliating Device

The DermaFlash ($189) is basically a gentle close-shaver for your face. It will leave your skin perfectly exfoliated and smooth after removing dead skin cells and built-up debris from the environment and makeup.

GHD Platinum+ Rose Gold Gift Set

The GHD Platinum+ styler got a rose gold makeover this holiday season. The GHD Platinum+ Rose Gold Gift Set ($249) is a lightweight hair straightener that has plates that adjusts its heat to your hair's thickness and how quickly you use the iron (so that you'll never apply too much heat to your hair.) It comes in a burgundy travel case.

GloPulse Deep & Quick Ion Enhancer

The GloPulse Deep & Quick Ion Enhancer ($165) increases blood circulation and decreases the appearance of fine lines and pores — all while making for an intriguing Instagram selfie.

Nurse Jamie Triangle Facial Beauty Tool

Use the Nurse Jamie Triangle Facial Beauty Tool ($159) — which you can choose to use at its normal or heated setting — to massage away puffiness.

Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare Pro Facial Steamer

The Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare Pro Facial Steamer ($149) creates a post-facial radiance in the comfort of your home. It works by "acting as a natural exfoliating treatment," according to its founder, to leave you with a clearer, glowing complexion.

Foreo Into the Deep Holiday Hydration Set

Foreo Into the Deep Holiday Hydration Set ($199) is a gift set that is sure to delight, as it contains an array of Tarte's hydrating skin care and the Foreo Luna 3, a silicone facial device that pulses to cleanse and massage skin.

Unplugged Beauty Cordless 1 Inch Curling Wand

The Unplugged Beauty Cordless 1 Inch Curling Wand ($99) will make storing and traveling with the hot tool that much easier with its lightweight and cordless body.
