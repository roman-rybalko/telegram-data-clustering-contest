HKFP Lens: Fierce police-protester battles around PolyU as students evacuate campus

19 Nov 2019, 22:07 by HKFP Lens

As of 11pm on Tuesday, around 800 people had left the Polytechnic University campus following a days-long battle between protesters and police. Some surrendered to police, whilst others escaped by other means. HKFP rounds up photos from United Social Press taken in nearby Jordan and Yau Ma Tei on Monday and Tuesday.
