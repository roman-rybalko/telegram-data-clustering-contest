Urvashi Rautela tries to recreate Aladdin's Princess Jasmine in her latest shoot

18 Nov 2019, 17:05 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Urvashi Rautela is currently busy promoting her upcoming film 'Pagalpanti' helmed by Anees Bazmee. The actress who is quite active on social media took to her Instagram to share some stunning pictures from her latest photo shoot!

In the pictures, she recreated Aladdin's princess Jasmine in the picture. She looked stunning in a blue off-shoulder top paired with a light blue tube skirt and yellow heels. The actress for this look for the promotional event of 'Pagalpanti'. She captioned the post, ""I'm like a shooting star, I've come so far. I can't go back to where I used to be."
