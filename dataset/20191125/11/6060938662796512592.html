Watch Video: Akshay Kumar and other 'Housefull 4' stars groove to Kriti Sanon-Arjun Kapoor's 'Panipat' song 'Mann Mein Shiva'

25 Nov 2019, 11:30 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Akshay Kumar and Kriti Sanon multi-starrer 'Housefull 4' which has entered the Rs 200 crore club saw all the stars of the film come together to celebrate the film's success along with producer Sajid Nadiadwala.

While Riteish Deshmukh, Kriti Kharbanda, Bobby Deol and Pooja Hegde celebrated the success along with Kriti's 'Pagalpanti' co-star and boyfriend Pulkit Samrat joining them, they all sent their best wishes for their co-star Kriti Sanon's upcoming film 'Panipat'.

The period drama which also stars Arjun Kapoor and Sanjay Dutt in pivotal roles will feature Kriti as Parvati Bai. Taking to Instagram, the diva shared a glimpse of all her co-stars along with producer Sajid grooving to the tunes of 'Panipat' song 'Mann Mein Shiva' and sending their best wishes to the entire team of 'Panipat'.
