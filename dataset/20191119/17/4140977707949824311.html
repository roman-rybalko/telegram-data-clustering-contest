New FIFA 20 patch make fans hopeful of improvement in standards of the game

19 Nov 2019, 17:02 by Tanish Chachra

New FIFA 20 patch make fans hopeful of improvement in the standards of the game, as certain elements in the game had severe faults.

FIFA 20 is set to include a new patch to the FIFA 20 to which several fans are hopeful that their issues will be resolved after several games detected faults in the latest football game released by EA Sports.

It is reported that major changes will be fixes for player switching, improvement to shot quality in the box as well as adding more fluidity to ground passing.
