Black Friday 2019 May Be Better Than Cyber Monday

19 Nov 2019, 22:03 by Rachel Epstein

Black Friday and Cyber Monday have become virtually interchangeable, which means it's become increasingly more difficult to decide whether to buy those Meghan Markle shoes while you're in a food coma...or wait until Monday. (I know, life is hard.) It's safe to assume the former before products sell out as most Black Friday deals extend through the weekend, ultimately just re-labeling themselves as Cyber Monday deals.

To avoid getting in trouble for sneaking away from your family too long while searching for your favorite brand's deals, we've rounded up the best fashion, accessories, tech, beauty, and home Black Friday sales, below.
