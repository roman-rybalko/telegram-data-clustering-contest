PSA: Unable to connect to Disney+? It’s not just you, it’s them… [U: Tweet]

12 Nov 2019, 13:18 by Ben Lovejoy

Disney+ launched slightly early today, the iOS and Android apps giving access ahead of the official launch at 3 am ET – but some subscribers have found themselves seeing an ‘Unable to connect to Disney+’ message.

Update: Disney has acknowledged the issue in a tweet, below …

The Verge reports on the Disney+ error message.

The launch day crunch is causing some problems for Disney+. After going live in the wee hours of the morning, Disney’s streaming service is now showing “unable to connect” errors as customers on the east coast begin waking up, signing up for their free trial, and exploring the Disney+ catalog.

Not everyone seems to be running into the errors, but reports are spiking across Twitter and other social media this morning.

The Disney+ Help account has now acknowledged the issue on Twitter.
